Summer Student Program
SGI
SGI’s Summer Student Program is more than just a job—it’s your first step toward a bright career with a company that invests in you. Whether you’re in high school or university, we’ve got opportunities for you to grow, learn, and make a meaningful impact.
If you’re driven, curious, and eager to make an impact, SGI is the place for you. The summer student term runs from May 1 to August 31, 2026, offering a unique opportunity to gain valuable experience and contribute meaningfully. We value diversity and welcome students from various programs, including but not limited to: Actuarial Science, Business, Health, Nursing, Psychology, Indigenous Studies, Trades, Math, Statistics, and IT.
